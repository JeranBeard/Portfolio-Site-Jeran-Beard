Paw Pals

Paw Pals is a tracking app for our furry loved ones. If you in your home town or on vacation Paw Pals will help you locate local groomers, Restaurants, Vets, Healthier food alternatives. It even goes as far as tracking your loved one incase they get lost and you can't find them.

New to owning a pet or travelling with a pet can be quite stressful. This app alleviates the stress and everything you could possibly need is at the tip of your fingers. The app allows users to have a form of relief and a lifeline is available if something were to happen. Every need is taken care of. Tools: Adobe XD

My Pigeonhole

Balancing work, school, family and social activities can be quite overwhelming and stressful. As a result a lot of people lack organisation and mozy through their day as it comes. Without having a game plan or a schedule for ourselves we become less productive in our present lives.

My Pigeonhole is a productivity app that allows users to create daily, short/long term goals and have the ability to track their progress on each one. It will help users be more organised and have structure in their day to day lives in a way that works best for them. Tools: Adobe XD
