PawPals

Track your fur-family, find pet-friendly restaurants, parks, nearby and emergency vets, healthy food retailers and more!




This is a UX/UI case study of a native IOS and Android mobile app.


Paw Pals is a tracking app for our furry loved ones. If you are in your home town or on vacation, Paw Pals will help you locate local groomers, restaurants, vets, healthy food alternatives. It even tracks your loved one in case they get lost, or you can’t find them.


Why Paw Pals?

New to owning a pet or travelling with a pet can be quite stressful. This app alleviates the stress and everything you could possibly need is at the tip of your fingers. The app allows users to have a form of relief and a lifeline is available if something were to happen. Every need is taken care of.

Problem

Owning a pet can be stressful if you unsure of which vet to take to them to if they fall ill or get injured. Finding a local park that allows dogs. Pet friendly restaurants can be a big issue as well. Maybe your dog has a specific diet and you not sure where to buy their food. Finding a well known trustworthy groomer. There is a lot to take into consideration when owning a pet and you need to ensure you know where places are in order to take off them.

Solution

Paw Pals is an app that allows you to create a medical ID for your pet. This can be used at vets if needed. Groomers/Mobile Groomers recommendations that are in the area will be available to you. Local restaurants and parks that allow pets are a feature too.

Final Design

This is the home page where you will be able to see notifications and reminders, configured in your settings. The design is fresh, clean and easy to use. Buttons are bright and easy to understand. The target audience is anyone with a precious pet, young owners as well as “empty-nesters” who often find primary companionship in a pet.


The idea around this page was to provide a medical aid card for the animal. It has all the relevant information needed and this helps frustration trying to remember everything. Along with near by vets if you are in need of one.


Some animals have strict diets that they need to stick to or maybe their owner only prefers them eating a certain type of food. In the diet section you have the option to buy and see recipes to make doggo treats and for those who are on a more strict eating plan have lots of choices for an alternative meal.


Track me is one of my favourite features. Not only does it help to find the nearest restaurant or dog park but you can actually connect a tracker on your dog so when they decide to go on adventure you have the ability to track them and see how far or where they are going.
